About Shivanjali

Crafting Timeless Elegance Since 2003

Welcome to Shivanjali Jewellers, where tradition meets modernity, and every piece tells a story of unmatched craftsmanship and timeless beauty. Established in 2003 by Uday Uttam Pawar, we have been proudly serving the people of Karad, Maharashtra, with the finest collection of Gold, Silver, and Diamond jewellery.

Our shop is located at Azad Chowk, Raviwar Peth, a hub of cultural richness, where we continue to build trust with each customer who walks through our doors.

At Shivanjali Jewellers, we understand the emotional value that jewellery holds. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, preserving a family tradition, or simply adding elegance to your everyday life, our stunning collections are designed to make every moment unforgettable.
